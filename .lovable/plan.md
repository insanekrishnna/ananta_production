

## Recommendations to Make Ananta Production Look Insanely Minimal and Premium

After reviewing your entire site, here's what I'd recommend to take it from "good agency site" to "this studio is on another level." The philosophy: **less decoration, more confidence. Let whitespace and typography do the talking.**

### 1. Smooth Page-Wide Scroll Animations
Right now sections pop in with basic fade-ups. Replace with buttery smooth scroll-triggered reveals using staggered timing and slight parallax — the kind of motion that makes people slow down and admire the craft.

### 2. Custom Cursor Effect
Add a custom circular cursor that scales up when hovering interactive elements (buttons, cards, links). This is a signature move of top-tier design agencies — immediately signals "we care about every pixel."

### 3. Hero Section — Strip It Down
- Remove the floating social media icons — they add visual noise
- Make the headline typography larger and bolder with tighter letter-spacing
- Add a subtle text reveal animation (words appearing one by one or characters sliding up)
- The app preview window is nice but could use a subtle parallax scroll effect

### 4. Horizontal Scrolling Case Studies Section
Replace or enhance the current "What We Build" expertise map with a horizontal-scrolling showcase of work/case studies. Full-bleed images with minimal text overlays. This is how elite agencies flex.

### 5. Marquee / Infinite Scroll Logo Strip
Replace the static logo pills with a smooth infinite-scrolling marquee — it feels alive and suggests momentum.

### 6. Number Counter Animations
The stats (96%, 20+, 78%) in the Analytics section should count up when they scroll into view rather than just appearing. Small detail, massive impact.

### 7. Dark Mode Toggle
Let visitors switch between light and dark. You already have `next-themes` installed. A dark mode with deep blacks and your indigo accent would look lethal.

### 8. Footer — Cleaner Layout
Reduce footer visual weight. Fewer links visible, more whitespace, larger brand mark.

| Priority | Feature | Impact |
